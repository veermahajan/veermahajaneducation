Problem Set 1 | Number Theory for Middle Schoolers

Click on each question to reveal the answer.

  1. Is 129 prime or composite?We can see that 129 is divisble by 3, so it is composite.
  2. How many proper divisors does 170 have?The divisors of 170 are 1, 2, 5, 10, 17, 34, 85, and 170, for a total of 8. 170 is not a proper divisor, so there are \(8-1=\)7 proper divisors.
  3. How many perfect squares lie between 1000 and 2000?We notice that \(32^2 = 1024\), and it is the smallest perfect square (\(31^2 = 961\), which is too little). Meanwhile, the largest such perfect square is \(44^2=1936\), as \(45^2=2025\) is too big. So, all perfect squares from 32 through 44 fit, giving us \(44-32+1=\) 13 perfect squares.
