Algebra for Middle Schoolers

  1. Techniques for Solving Equations
    • Video
    • Summary
      • Learn how to solve one-variable equations through isolation, where a variable is moved to one side of the equation with constants on the other.
      • Discover methods to solve systems of equations with more than one variable, including elimination, which can greatly increase speed in problem-solving.
      • Understand that larger systems can be solved with substitution and elimination, but problems often require clever manipulations rather than this, while also learning key vocabulary terms such as consistent, inconsistent, dependent/redundant, and independent.
      • When you feel ready, attempt the problems in the practice section. These range from elementary questions with basic concepts to tough, AIME-level questions testing applications and creativity. Full solutions are also available to check your work.
