Trigonometry

Trigonometry is one of the most widely applied branches of mathematics, and is used in many aspects of daily life. Starting from simple right-triangle trigonometry, learn various formulas and rules that help you connect it to the world around you! From the famous \(sin^2(\theta)+ cos^2(\theta) = 1\) to the lesser-known \(sin{\theta \over 2} = \pm \sqrt{{1 - cos(\theta)} \over 2}\), learn how the trigonometric functions are related and how they can be applied to real life.
